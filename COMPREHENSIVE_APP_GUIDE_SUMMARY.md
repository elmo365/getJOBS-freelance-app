## Language & Terminology

### Simple Explanations

**Escrow** = Platform holds money safely until job done
**Verification** = Proof that person/company is real
**Moderation** = Admin reviewing content for safety
**Suspension** = Temporarily blocking someone from platform
**Dispute** = Disagreement between parties that needs resolution
**Application** = Job seeker applying for a job
**Shortlist** = Moving good candidates to next round
**Audit Log** = Record of all admin actions
**Widget** = Small feature or tool in app
**API** = System for connecting apps together

**CV** = Curriculum Vitae (resume)
**AI** = Artificial Intelligence

**ID** = Identification
