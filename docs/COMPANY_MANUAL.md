## Company Verification (KYC)

**Important:** You must verify your company before posting jobs.

### Required Documents

Upload the following documents for verification:

1. **CIPA Certificate** (PDF/DOC)
   - Certificate of Incorporation from CIPA
   - Must be current and valid

2. **CIPA Extract** (PDF/DOC)
   - Recent extract from CIPA showing company status
   - Should be dated within the last 6 months

3. **BURS TIN Evidence** (PDF/JPG/PNG)
   - Tax Identification Number certificate
   - Proof of tax registration

4. **Proof of Business Address** (PDF/JPG/PNG)
   - Utility bill, lease agreement, or bank statement
   - Must show business address matching registration

### Optional Documents

- **Authority Letter / Resolution** (PDF/DOC)
  - If registering on behalf of the company
  - Board resolution authorizing registration

### Verification Process

1. Go to **Company Verification** from your dashboard
2. Upload all required documents
3. Review your submissions
4. Tap "Submit for Review"
5. Wait for admin approval (typically 1-3 business days)
6. You'll receive a notification when approved

### Verification Status

- **Pending:** Documents submitted, awaiting review
- **Under Review:** Admin is reviewing your documents
- **Approved:** ‚úÖ You can now post jobs
- **Rejected:** Review rejection reason and resubmit

**Note:** You cannot post jobs until your company is verified and approved.

## Posting Jobs

### Creating a Job Posting

1. Go to **Post a Job** from your dashboard
2. Fill in job details:

   **Basic Information:**
   - **Job Title** (required) - e.g., "Software Developer"
   - **Category** (required) - IT, Healthcare, Finance, etc.
   - **Location** - City, country, or "Remote"
   - **Job Type** - Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship

   **Compensation:**
   - **Salary Range** - Minimum and maximum (BWP)
   - **Benefits** - Health insurance, bonuses, etc.

   **Job Details:**
   - **Description** - Detailed job description
   - **Requirements** - Skills, education, experience needed
   - **Responsibilities** - Key duties and tasks
   - **Experience Level** - Entry, Mid, Senior

   **Additional:**
   - **Application Deadline** (optional)
   - **Start Date** (optional)
   - **Remote Work Options**

3. **Review** your posting
4. **Publish** to make it live

## Interview Scheduling

### Scheduling an Interview

1. Go to **Schedule Interview** or select a candidate
2. Choose **Date and Time**
3. Select **Interview Type:**
   - In-person
   - Video call (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
   - Phone call
4. Add **Interview Details:**
   - Location or video link
   - Interview format (technical, behavioral, etc.)
   - Interviewer names
5. Add **Notes** (optional)
6. **Send Invitation**

### Managing Interviews

- **View All Interviews:** See all scheduled interviews
- **Reschedule:** Change date/time if needed
- **Cancel:** Cancel interview with notification
- **Add Notes:** Record interview feedback
- **Mark Complete:** After interview is done

### Interview Best Practices

1. **Confirm Early:** Send invitations at least 3 days in advance
2. **Provide Details:** Include location, format, and what to expect
3. **Follow Up:** Send reminders 24 hours before
4. **Take Notes:** Record feedback during/after interview
5. **Communicate:** Update candidates on next steps

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Post Jobs

**Possible reasons:**
- Company not verified/approved
- Missing required information
- Account issues

**Solutions:**
- Check verification status
- Complete company profile
- Contact support if verified but still can't post

### Applications Not Showing

- Refresh the applications list
- Check internet connection
- Verify job is still active
- Contact support if issue persists

### Interview Scheduling Issues

- Check date/time availability
- Verify candidate contact information
- Ensure notifications are enabled
- Try rescheduling if needed

### Documents Not Uploading

- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Use supported formats (PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG)
- Check internet connection
- Try smaller file sizes
