## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Download the App** from the App Store or Play Store
2. **Open the App** and tap "Register"
3. **Choose Account Type:**
   - **Job Seeker** - Looking for employment opportunities
   - **Employer/Company** - Hiring talent for your organization
4. **Fill in your details:**
   - Full Name (or Company Name for employers)
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Phone Number
   - Address
5. **Upload Profile Photo** (optional but recommended)
6. **Tap "Create Account"**

### Logging In

1. Open the app
2. Tap "Login"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Tap "Sign In"

## For Job Seekers

### Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:
- **Recommended Jobs** - AI-powered suggestions based on your profile
- **Quick Stats** - Applications, Views, and Match Score
- **Quick Access** - CV Builder, Job Search, AI Interview Coach, and more

### Building Your CV

1. Go to **Digital CV Builder** from the dashboard
2. Fill in each section:
   - Personal Information
   - Professional Summary
   - Work Experience
   - Education
   - Skills
   - References
3. Preview and export as PDF

### Searching for Jobs

1. Tap **Browse Jobs** or use the Search icon
2. Use filters:
   - **Keywords** - Job title, company, or skills
   - **Location** - City, country, or "remote"
   - **Category** - IT, Healthcare, Finance, etc.
   - **Experience Level** - Entry, Mid, Senior
3. Tap on a job to view details
4. Tap "Apply" to submit your application

### Applying for Jobs

1. Find a job you're interested in
2. Tap "Apply Now"
3. Review your profile/CV
4. Add a cover message (optional)
5. Submit your application
6. Track status in "My Applications"

### Video Resume

Stand out with a video introduction:
1. Go to **Video Resume** from the dashboard
2. Tap "Record New"
3. Follow the tips for a professional video
4. Upload and attach to your profile

### Career Tracker

Monitor your job search progress:
- Applications submitted
- Interview invitations
- Response rates
- Weekly activity

### AI Interview Coach ü§ñ

Practice for interviews with AI-powered coaching:

1. Go to **AI Interview Coach** from the dashboard
2. Enter the job title you're applying for
3. Select your experience level (Entry, Mid, Senior)
4. Get tailored interview questions based on the role
5. Practice answering each question
6. Get AI feedback with:
   - Score (1-10)
   - Strengths in your answer
   - Areas for improvement
   - Suggested improved answer
7. Review all questions and practice until confident

**Tips:**
- Be specific and use examples (STAR method)
- Practice regularly for better performance
- Review feedback to improve your answers

### AI-Powered Job Matching

The app uses AI to:
- **Match your skills** to job requirements
- **Score compatibility** (0-100%)
- **Rank recommendations** by best fit
- **Learn from your behavior** (jobs you view, apply to, etc.)

Your dashboard shows "Recommended Jobs" ranked by AI match score.

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Loading
- Check your internet connection
- Force close and reopen the app
- Update to the latest version

### Can't Login
- Verify email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Check for caps lock on password

### Job Application Failed
- Ensure profile is complete
- Check internet connection
- Try again in a few minutes

### Documents Not Uploading
- Reduce file size (max 10MB)
- Use supported formats (PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG)
- Check internet speed
