Company Verification (KYC)

Important: You must verify your company before posting jobs.

Required Documents

Upload the following documents for verification:

  1. CIPA Certificate (PDF/DOC)
    • Certificate of Incorporation from CIPA
    • Must be current and valid
  2. CIPA Extract (PDF/DOC)
    • Recent extract from CIPA showing company status
    • Should be dated within the last 6 months
  3. BURS TIN Evidence (PDF/JPG/PNG)
    • Tax Identification Number certificate
    • Proof of tax registration
  4. Proof of Business Address (PDF/JPG/PNG)
    • Utility bill, lease agreement, or bank statement
    • Must show business address matching registration

Optional Documents

  • Authority Letter / Resolution (PDF/DOC)
    • If registering on behalf of the company
    • Board resolution authorizing registration

Verification Process

  1. Go to Company Verification from your dashboard
  2. Upload all required documents
  3. Review your submissions
  4. Tap ‚ÄúSubmit for Review‚Äù
  5. Wait for admin approval (typically 1-3 business days)
  6. You‚Äôll receive a notification when approved

Verification Status

  • Pending: Documents submitted, awaiting review
  • Under Review: Admin is reviewing your documents
  • Approved: ‚úÖ You can now post jobs
  • Rejected: Review rejection reason and resubmit

Note: You cannot post jobs until your company is verified and approved.

Interview Scheduling Issues

  • Check date/time availability
  • Verify candidate contact information

  • Check internet connection
