AI-Powered Job Matching

What it does: Analyzes your CV and finds jobs that match your skills and experience.

How to Use:

  1. Navigate to Smart Job Matching:
  2. From Home Screen ‚Üí Tap "Smart Job Matching"

  3. Or from Profile ‚Üí "AI Job Recommendations"

  4. How It Works:

  5. The AI analyzes your CV automatically
  6. Compares your skills with job requirements
  7. Calculates a match score (0-100%)

  8. Shows top matching jobs

  9. Understanding Match Scores:

  10. 90-100%: Excellent match - highly recommended
  11. 75-89%: Good match - strong candidate
  12. 60-74%: Moderate match - some skills may be missing

  13. Below 60%: Low match - may need more experience

  14. View Recommendations:

  15. See match score for each job
  16. Read AI reasoning for the match

  17. See which skills match and which are missing

  18. Apply: Tap "View Details" ‚Üí "Apply Now"

‚ùì Troubleshooting

Can't Log In

Problem: Forgot password or account locked Solution: 1. Tap "Forgot Password" 2. Enter your email 3. Check email for reset link 4. Create new password 5. Log in with new password

Application Not Submitting

Problem: Error when applying Solution: 1. Check internet connection 2. Make sure you're logged in 3. Complete your CV first 4. Try again 5. If persists, contact support

Not Receiving Notifications

Problem: Missing notifications Solution: 1. Check app notification permissions (Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí BotsJobsConnect ‚Üí Notifications) 2. Check email spam folder 3. Verify email address in profile 4. Make sure app is updated

AI Matching Not Working

Problem: No job recommendations Solution: 1. Complete your CV (minimum: skills, experience, education) 2. Wait a few minutes for analysis 3. Refresh the screen 4. Check if jobs are available in your category

Can't View Application Status

Problem: Application history empty or not loading Solution: 1. Check internet connection 2. Pull down to refresh 3. Make sure you've applied to at least one job 4. Log out and log back in
