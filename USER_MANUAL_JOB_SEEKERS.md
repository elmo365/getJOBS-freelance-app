## üë§ Building Your Profile

### Digital CV Builder

**Why it's important**: Your CV is your first impression. Build a complete CV to get better job matches.

**How to Build Your CV**:

1. **Navigate to CV Builder**:
   - From Home Screen ‚Üí Tap "Digital CV Builder"
   - Or from Profile ‚Üí Tap "Build CV"

2. **Fill in Sections**:
   - **Personal Information**: Name, contact details, location
   - **Professional Summary**: Write a brief summary of your skills and experience
   - **Work Experience**: 
     - Add each job: Company, Position, Duration, Responsibilities
     - Start with most recent
   - **Education**: 
     - Add degrees, certifications
     - Include institution, year, field of study
   - **Skills**: 
     - List technical and soft skills
     - Be specific (e.g., "Flutter Development", "Project Management")
   - **Languages**: Languages you speak and proficiency level
   - **Additional Sections**:
     - Certifications
     - Awards
     - Projects
     - References

3. **Save Your CV**: Tap "Save" after each section

4. **Preview**: Tap "Preview CV" to see how it looks

5. **Export**: Tap "Export PDF" to download your CV

### Video Resume

**What it is**: A short video introducing yourself to employers.

**How to Create**:

1. Navigate to "Video Resume" from Home Screen
2. Tap "Record Video Resume"
3. **Tips for Recording**:
   - Good lighting
   - Quiet environment
   - Professional attire
   - Keep it 1-2 minutes
   - Introduce yourself, highlight key skills
4. Tap "Stop" when done
5. Review and tap "Save" or "Re-record"
6. Your video will be attached to applications automatically

## üîç Finding Jobs

### Browse Jobs

1. **From Home Screen**:
   - Tap "Browse Jobs"
   - Or use the Search tab at the bottom

2. **Search Features**:
   - **Search Bar**: Type keywords (job title, company, skills)
   - **Filters**:
     - Category (IT, Marketing, etc.)
     - Location
     - Job Type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract)
     - Experience Level (Entry, Mid, Senior)
     - Salary Range

3. **View Job Details**:
   - Tap any job card
   - See full description, requirements, company info
   - Check application deadline

### AI-Powered Job Matching

**What it does**: Analyzes your CV and finds jobs that match your skills and experience.

**How to Use**:

1. **Navigate to Smart Job Matching**:
   - From Home Screen ‚Üí Tap "Smart Job Matching"
   - Or from Profile ‚Üí "AI Job Recommendations"

2. **How It Works**:
   - The AI analyzes your CV automatically
   - Compares your skills with job requirements
   - Calculates a match score (0-100%)
   - Shows top matching jobs

3. **Understanding Match Scores**:
   - **90-100%**: Excellent match - highly recommended
   - **75-89%**: Good match - strong candidate
   - **60-74%**: Moderate match - some skills may be missing
   - **Below 60%**: Low match - may need more experience

4. **View Recommendations**:
   - See match score for each job
   - Read AI reasoning for the match
   - See which skills match and which are missing

5. **Apply**: Tap "View Details" ‚Üí "Apply Now"

## üìù Applying for Jobs

### How to Apply

1. **Find a Job**: Browse or use AI matching
2. **View Job Details**: Tap the job card
3. **Review Requirements**: Make sure you meet the criteria
4. **Tap "Apply Now"**:
   - Your CV will be automatically attached
   - Your video resume (if available) will be included
   - Application is submitted instantly

5. **Confirmation**: You'll see "Application submitted successfully!"

### What Happens After Applying

1. **Immediate**: 
   - Application status: "Pending"
   - Employer receives notification
   - You receive confirmation

2. **Review Process**:
   - Employer reviews your application
   - AI analyzes your match score
   - Employer makes decision

3. **Possible Outcomes**:
   - **Approved**: You'll get a notification
   - **Interview Scheduled**: You'll receive interview details
   - **Rejected**: You'll receive feedback (if provided)

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Forgot password or account locked
**Solution**:
1. Tap "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new password

### Application Not Submitting

**Problem**: Error when applying
**Solution**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Make sure you're logged in
3. Complete your CV first
4. Try again
5. If persists, contact support

### Not Receiving Notifications

**Problem**: Missing notifications
**Solution**:
1. Check app notification permissions (Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí BotsJobsConnect ‚Üí Notifications)
2. Check email spam folder
3. Verify email address in profile
4. Make sure app is updated

### AI Matching Not Working

**Problem**: No job recommendations
**Solution**:
1. Complete your CV (minimum: skills, experience, education)
2. Wait a few minutes for analysis
3. Refresh the screen
4. Check if jobs are available in your category

### Can't View Application Status

**Problem**: Application history empty or not loading
**Solution**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh
3. Make sure you've applied to at least one job
4. Log out and log back in
